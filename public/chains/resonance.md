## resonance testnet

resonance is a public canary network, implementing bleeding edge feature-sets for post quantum blockchains.

### known public endpoints

endpoints listed below are known to maintain a public status document where historical downtime is recorded and observable.

- [a.t.res.fm](https://polkadot.js.org/apps/?rpc=wss%3A%2F%2Fa.t.res.fm#/explorer)
  - [status](https://status.res.fm)
  - rpc: `https://a.t.res.fm`
  - websocket: `wss://a.t.res.fm`
